Consignment

Have musical equipment you want to sell? We offer consignment!

Consignment for consignor of music equipment
  1. Consignor is responsible for insuring all consigned merchandise. Topper's will not be liable for any loss, including but not limited to fire, theft, or water damage.
  2. Consignor agrees that he or she will receive:
    • 65% of the sale price and that Topper's will receive 35% of the sales price for merchandise of $100 or less.
    • 75% of the sale price and that Topper's will receive 25% of the sales price for merchandise from $101 to $200.
    • 80% of the sale price and that Topper's will receive 20% of the sales price for merchandise from $201 to $700.
    • 85% of the sale price and that Topper's will receive 15% of the sales price for merchandise over $701.
  3. Consignor may at anytime reclaim merchandise

Consignment for purchaser of consignment music equipment
The undersigned "Purchaser" and Representative of "Topper's Music, LLC" agree to the following terms:
  1. The Purchaser understands that the equipment listed below which he or she is buying was acquired by Topper's Music, LLC on consignment.
  2. Topper's Music, LLC provides absolutely no guarantee or warranty on consigned equipment
