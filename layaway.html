Layaway

Lay-away policy and agreement
  • Minimum down payment on lay-away items is 20% of purchase including tax.
  • The maximum time to pay lay-away off is 60 days from the date of lay-away origination.
  • NO REFUNDS will be given on lay-away items as they have been off the sales floor and the store has lost opportunities to sell it to other customers.
  • After 60 days of lay-away origination, items will be returned to the sales floor, for sale. Customer forfeits all of down payment and other payments made. The purpose of lay-away is for you to set something back that you really want and avoid someone else buying it.
